How Stuff Works

Challenge

This is the work of Aidan Walb, Carlee Stone, Timohty Rosario, and Raymond Hall for their current challenge How Stuff Works.
The purpose of the challenge is to take apart common household appliancences and view their internals to see How Stuff Works.
The group decided to take apart a microwave due to its complicated parts like the magnetron and capacitor.
