Magnetron

A magnetron has a filament that is contained inside a vacuum chamber.
The filament is heated to the point where it starts to excite electrons that start to bounce around the
chambers in which they are contained which creates a small magnetic field.
The magnet that is below the filament causes the electrons to spin around in the chamber.
Depending on the voltage that is being applied, they spin faster or slower.

Touch Panel

The touch panel allows you to decide how long, and style. This is done by using the number pad on the side of the microwave.

This fan cools the components within the microwave so they do not overheat.
